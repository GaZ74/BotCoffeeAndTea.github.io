Our Story

Nestled near the beach, Bọt Coffee & Tea is one of the most loved coffee spots in Vũng Tàu — a place where people come to relax, take pictures, and enjoy a cup of coffee with ocean breeze. The shop is decorated with gentle tones and natural light, creating a cozy and stylish atmosphere that’s perfect for both quiet mornings and social hangouts.

Every drink here is made with care — from rich espresso-based coffee to refreshing fruit tea. Many customers come not only for the taste but also for the friendly service and the many “Instagram-worthy” corners throughout the shop.
